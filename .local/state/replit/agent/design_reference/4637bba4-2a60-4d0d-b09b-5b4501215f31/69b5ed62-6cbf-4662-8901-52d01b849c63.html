Event Instructions

Everything you need to know to participate and excel in Code Vault hackathon.

1

Registration

Complete online registration with team details. Individual participants will be assigned to teams during the event.

2

Check-in

Arrive by 9:00 AM on September 19th. Bring valid ID, laptop, chargers, and any development tools you prefer.

3

Team Setup

Finalize teams (2-4 members max). Solo participants will be matched based on skills and interests.

4

Problem Release

AI-focused problem statements revealed at 10:00 AM. Choose your challenge and start coding immediately.

5

Coding Marathon

24-hour development phase with mentorship available. Meals, snacks, and refreshments provided throughout.

6

Demo & Judging

Submit projects by 9:00 AM on September 20th. Present 5-minute demos to expert judges for evaluation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a team to participate?

No, individual registrations are welcome. We'll help match you with suitable teammates.

What should I bring?

Laptop, chargers, valid ID, and enthusiasm! We'll provide food, drinks, and workspace.

Is there an entry fee?

The event is completely free for all registered participants. Just bring your ideas!

Can beginners participate?

Absolutely! We welcome all skill levels and provide mentorship throughout the event.
